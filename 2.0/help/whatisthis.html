The «Paving Design Expert» software is intended to develop projects for paving tiles. It can help you introduce to and coordinate the pavement projects with your customers.

This program gives you ability to:
·Use tiles of any shape or create new shapes, not present in the library;  
·Set mutual alignment while paving tiles and create different patterns;  
·Change the coloring of any tile in the pattern;  
·View tiles stats, i.e. the number of used tiles (wholes and halves) divided by color;  
·Create paving projects for arbitrary shape plots and voids inside.  
·Print projects.
